# Confetti Cloud
![[systems-pf2e-icons-spells-confetti-cloud.webp|150]]

You conjure a dense storm of swirling confetti. All creatures within the area of the confetti become [[Concealed]], and all creatures outside the storm become concealed to creatures within it.
